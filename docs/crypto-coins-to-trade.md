### 1. Coinbase Derivatives (US-Regulated Futures)

**Traditional Expiry Futures:**
* **Nano Bitcoin (BIT):** 1/100th of a Bitcoin
* **Nano Ether (ET):** 1/10th of an Ether
* **Bitcoin Cash (BCH):** 1 BCH per contract
* **Litecoin (LTC):** 5 LTC per contract
* **Dogecoin (DOGE):** 5,000 DOGE per contract
* **Other Altcoins:** Coinbase has also launched or filed to list traditional futures for **Polkadot (DOT)**, **Avalanche (AVA)**, **Chainlink (LNK)**, **Stellar (XLM)**, **Shiba Inu (1k SHIB)**, and **Hedera**. 

**US Perpetual-Style Futures:**
* **Nano Bitcoin Perpetual-Style (BIP):** 0.01 BTC
* **Nano Ether Perpetual-Style (ETP):** 0.10 ETH
* **Nano Solana Perpetual-Style (SLP):** 5 SOL
* **Nano XRP Perpetual-Style (XPP):** 500 XRP

**Crypto-Adjacent Index Futures:**
* **Mag7 + Crypto Equity Index (MC):** A hybrid contract that offers equal-weighted, combined exposure to the traditional "Magnificent 7" tech stocks alongside two US spot crypto ETFs (BlackRock's IBIT for Bitcoin and ETHA for Ethereum). 

### 2. Coinbase International Exchange (Non-US Perpetuals)
For institutional and retail customers outside the US, the UK, and Canada, Coinbase International Exchange offers standard **Perpetual Futures**. 

Because these aren't restricted by US derivatives regulations, the list of available assets is massive and constantly expanding. It includes perpetual contracts for dozens of major cryptocurrencies, such as:
* **Blue Chips:** Bitcoin (BTC-PERP), Ethereum (ETH-PERP), Solana (SOL-PERP)
* **DeFi & Layer 2s:** Optimism (OP-PERP), Arbitrum (ARB-PERP), Injective (INJ-PERP), Uniswap (UNI-PERP)
* **Altcoins & Memes:** Avalanche (AVAX-PERP), Cardano (ADA-PERP), Dogecoin (DOGE-PERP), Aptos (APT-PERP), Sui (SUI-PERP), Filecoin (FIL-PERP), and many more.
